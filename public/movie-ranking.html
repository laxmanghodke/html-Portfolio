The Best Movie According to Laxman

My Top 3 movies of all time

Persuite of happness

The film stars Will Smith as Chris Gardner and his real-life son Jaden Smith as Christopher Jr., portraying Gardner's nearly one-year struggle with homelessness in San Francisco in 1981. The story is based on Gardner's memoir and highlights his determination to overcome financial hardship while raising his young son alone. The title's intentional misspelling, "Happyness," comes from a mural Gardner sees outside his son's daycare.

Hey jawani he deewani

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani revolves around four former classmates who meet at a trekking trip where Naina Talwar, a shy medical student, falls in love with carefree brat Kabir "Bunny" Thapar but refrains from expressing her feelings. They soon drift apart but end up meeting again at mutual friend Aditi Mehra's wedding.

hasee toh phasee

Hasee Toh Phasee is a 2014 Hindi romantic comedy starring Sidharth Malhotra and Parineeti Chopra, following a quirky love story during wedding chaos. Explore reviews, cast details, and streaming options on Netflix and Apple TV.
